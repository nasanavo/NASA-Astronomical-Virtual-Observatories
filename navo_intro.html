These pages describe the work of the NASA Astronomical Virtual Observatories (NAVO), a collaboration of NASA's astronomy archives, who have developed a comprehensive model for distributing data through standardized machine-queryable interfaces. Scientists looking to get data may can go directly to our NAVO services page to learn how to use these. We also provide an introduction to the virtual observatory interfaces used, the details of the mission implementation, and the other activities of NAVO.
